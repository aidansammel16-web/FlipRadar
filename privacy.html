FlipRadar Privacy Policy

Data Collection

FlipRadar operates entirely on your local device. It stores:

  • Marketplace search URLs you configure.
  • IDs of listings already seen, to prevent duplicate alerts.
  • A short history of recent alerts (up to 50) for display in the popup.
  • Your scanner settings (check interval, minimum score).

External Communication

The extension loads Facebook Marketplace pages in browser tabs to read listing data from your configured searches. No data is transmitted to any third-party server, analytics service, or the extension developer.

Storage

All data is stored locally via chrome.storage.local. Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.

Permissions

  • storage — persist searches, settings, and seen-item IDs.
  • notifications — show desktop alerts for qualifying deals.
  • alarms — schedule periodic scans.
  • tabs — open and manage Marketplace tabs for scanning.
  • scripting — inject the content script to extract listing data.
  • host_permissions (facebook.com) — required to read Marketplace pages.
