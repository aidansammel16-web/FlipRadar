Privacy Policy

FlipRadar does not collect or store personal data such as names, emails, or passwords.

The extension may store the following locally or in our database:

  • Search URLs configured by the user
  • Keywords and filters
  • A randomly generated device identifier
  • Subscription status (Pro vs Free)


No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Payments are securely handled by Stripe. FlipRadar does not store payment information.
